Environmentally friendly production

Renewable energy

We source electricity from solar panels installed at our facility. Up to 80% of our electricity consumption is generated onsite. Our industrial light systems are energy efficient and our buildings are insulated.

Lean production

We regularly examine our production processes to ensure that material leftovers are reduced to an absolute minimum.

Waste management & recycling

All packaging materials such as cardboard, paper and plastic are sorted and stored onsite before being shipped to a nearby recycling facility.

Non-toxic components

We use only certified non-toxic chemical products such as glues and polishes which are harmless to health and the environment.

Environmental, Social, and Governance Criteria

We fully implement european standards for environmental protection, health & safety and labour
